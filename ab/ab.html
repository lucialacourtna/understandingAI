About Us

Understanding AI is a nonprofit project aiming to improve the literacy of AI for the general public. We aim to give people the information required for them to develop informed opinions and decisions regarding this technology. Additionally, we aim to improve literacy at a governmental level, and to urge politicians to educate themselves on what is becoming a huge legal issue.
