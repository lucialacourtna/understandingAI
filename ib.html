The Dangers of Bias In AI

AI trains on largely unfiltered datasets from all over the web. Given the amount of racial and gender bias still prevalent around the world, it is inevitable that the AI training off of human sources will internalize some of it. This can cause dangerous consequences as AI begins to be implemented in general society. AI tools have already perpetuated several instances of housing, hiring, and financial lending discrimination.
